UFC 254



UFC 254 had one of the most interesting fights this year. Khabib "The Eagle" Nurmagomedovthe returining Lightweight Champion defended his title against his most dangerous opponent Justin "The Highlight" Gaethje
Coming into the bout, this fight had all the tell tale signs of an upset with Khabib struggling to make weight on the scale and his walk out. But the reality couldn't have been far from it. In a most stunning turn, Khabib brought the All American NCAA wrestler to his back and almost secured a scary arm bar at the end of the third. Even at standup Gaethje a usually scrappy and pocket fighter seemed hesitant. An all emerging trend with new contenders for titles.
Finally in the second round in an almost perfect fight, Khabib displayed his new but welcome Jiu Jitsu set and transitioned smoothly to mount, going for an arm bar and finallu a triangle putting the Highlight to sleep.
After which, he gave a heartfelt speech and retired from a sport which he caused huge waves in, cementing his position for GOAT status with a record of 29-0 among the elites like George St. Pierre, Jon Jones and Demetrius Johnson.
